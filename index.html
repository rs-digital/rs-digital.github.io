h hi there, I'm Ryan. Thanks for stoppin' by.

What you should expect to find here on my page is a variety of my artistic skill and talent. I enjoy creating things. Whether it's on a computer or a piece of paper, music or coding, illustrating or just daydreaming--I always have the need to come up with something to make.

I built this page to show you a little bit of my style and medium. I am fluent in Adobe Photoshop, and pretty good with Illustrator. I also tend to use Corel Paint, Adobe Draw, Adobe Shape, and a bit of InDesign when needed.

I have most recently been learning to code in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and a little PHP. Bootstrap and JQuery are also getting more familiar to me. It's been a great experience attending classes at BitWise Industries here in Fresno, Califonia, and I plan on learning a great deal more.

I've done some work for various musical artists as well as for my own side project, RS.Digital. You'll see some other random pieces here too.

The photos you see here range from when I first started snapping away with my Rebel, up until just recently. People, animals, nature, cars-- I try and capture it all.

Most of these digital drawings come from two fantasty-style games (one created by me, and one that wasn't).

I currently don't have a ton to show on this area, but the page you are looking at right now was built by me. Nothing too fancy, just a simple, clean site that is easy to navigate and nice to look at.
